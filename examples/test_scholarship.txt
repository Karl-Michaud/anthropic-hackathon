The Black North Initiative Award

Awarded on the basis of academic merit and demonstrated leadership excellence (including, but not limited to arts, sports, music, 
entrepreneurship or social enterprise) to domestic students coming from Canadian secondary schools and entering any 
first entry undergraduate program. Financial need may also be considered for these awards.
Eligible candidates must have a minimum admission average of 95%.

Value: $20,000 (non-renewable)
Deadline : March 5th, 2024
