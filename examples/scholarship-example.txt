SCHOLARSHIP NAME:
Tech Innovators Scholarship 2025

SCHOLARSHIP DETAILS:
This scholarship offers $5,000 to students who demonstrate exceptional creativity and innovation in technical projects. The application deadline is March 15, 2025.

Eligibility requirements include:
- Must be enrolled in a STEM degree program (Computer Science, Engineering, etc.)
- Minimum GPA of 3.5 on a 4.0 scale
- Must be a full-time undergraduate or graduate student
- Demonstrated experience with technical projects or research

The scholarship values hands-on experience and real-world impact. We are looking for students who aren't afraid to experiment, iterate, and learn from failure. Selection criteria emphasize innovation and creativity in technical problem-solving (40%), technical achievement and depth (35%), demonstration of resilience through failure (15%), and impact and real-world application (10%).

ESSAY PROMPT:
Describe your most innovative technical project (500-700 words). Explain the challenges you faced, how you overcame them, and the measurable impact your work has had. We want to hear about projects that didn't go as planned and how you iterated on your ideas.
