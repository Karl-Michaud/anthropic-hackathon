Tech Innovators Scholarship 2025

Award Amount: $5,000

Application Deadline: March 15, 2025

Description:
The Tech Innovators Scholarship is designed to support students who demonstrate exceptional creativity and innovation in technical projects. We are looking for students who aren't afraid to experiment, iterate, and learn from failure.

This scholarship values hands-on experience and real-world impact. Tell us about your most innovative technical project, the challenges you faced, and how you overcame them.

Eligibility Requirements:
- Must be enrolled in a STEM degree program (Computer Science, Engineering, etc.)
- Minimum GPA of 3.5 on a 4.0 scale
- Must be a full-time undergraduate or graduate student
- Demonstrated experience with technical projects or research
- Must submit a portfolio of technical work

Application Requirements:
- Essay describing your most innovative technical project (500-700 words)
- Academic transcript
- Two letters of recommendation from faculty or industry professionals
- Portfolio of technical projects (GitHub, website, etc.)

About the Scholarship:
We believe that innovation comes from taking risks and learning from mistakes. We want to hear about projects that didn't go as planned, how you iterated on your ideas, and the measurable impact your work has had.

Selection Criteria:
- Innovation and creativity in technical problem-solving (40%)
- Technical achievement and depth (35%)
- Demonstration of resilience through failure (15%)
- Impact and real-world application (10%)
